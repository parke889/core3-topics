Wall of Want is a digital experience that invites you to immerse yourself in the overwhelming abundance of products that fill our modern world. Inspired by the chaotic beauty of a poster-covered wall, this website fragments text from a publication project and combines it with striking visual elements to create a visually engaging and thought-provoking exploration of consumer culture. Scroll through a seemingly endless stream of products and contemplate the impact of throwaway culture, the rapid cycle of modern industries, and the pervasive influence of consumerism on our lives. As an extension of the previous publication project, it is taking some pieces of text from the book and fragmenting them with additional design pieces in the way that echoes posters on the walls. Enjoy scrolling through this flood of products exhisting in this world and see which one you would like to consume!
